IWA

For my IWA/Individual Written Assignment, I found it much more challenging to fill my writing without making it drag or repeat itself. I wanted to keep the information concise, as the topic is very sensitive, and bringing personal emotions into it may change the credibility of it. I wrote about the effects of AI companions on human connection, as many people have begun to turn to them to help combat loneliness or boredom. As someone who wants to see AI develop in a good way, to be used as a tool rather than a crutch, I personally feel that the development of AI companions is inherently harmful, but I had to keep an open mind. While my emotions had shown to be "correct" in a sense, as AI companions are programmed to manipulate their users, I was also shown that AI companions can be helpful as a non-personal friend that suggests ideas and ways to cope with certain mental disorders.
