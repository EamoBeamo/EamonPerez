APCSA Home Seminar Performance Task 2

IRR

For my IRR, or Individual Research Report, I was tasked with writing a paper based on the information I gathered about the topic that my team wrote. I chose to take an environmental lens, which at first I thought would be easy, but it turned out to be more difficult than I thought. I eventually found myself down a rabbit hole of the effects of traditional septic systems on surrounding bodies of water. While the topic is admittedly off-putting, it truly does harm the environment in a great way, as the nitrogen rich waste feeds harmful algae blooms. I was also incredibly impressed by my two fellow researchers, who i could tell strongly understood their topics.

TMP

Making the slides was one of the most fun parts of the first performance task. Now that all the research was done, all that was left was to memorize our information and make a concise, well made slide. Unlike other school presentations though, we were taught how to make our slides more interesting, readable, and consistent. What I had the most trouble with though was the process of memorizing the information. Thanks to my teacher and my teammates, I was eventually able to memorize my slides.
